Swarm

Reference Work

Neighbourly

https://www.neighbourly.co.nz/

New Zealand based website to also help communities connect through either helpful community posts or urgent alerts.

While Swarm is similar to this, it streamlines the different uses of neighborly into different tags and offers a new intriguing aesthetic. Swarm is also only for Wellington residents.

Reddit

https://www.reddit.com/

Reddit is made up of different communities where anonymous users can post with different tags specifying the nature of their content.

Taking inspiration from this feature, Swarm allows users to specify their post content with tags that users can search by.
