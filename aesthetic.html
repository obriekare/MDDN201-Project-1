Swarm

Aesthetic

Swarm takes a warm yellow, orange and brown colour palette. It is designed to feel as welcoming and pleasing to look at as possible. The main logo and title font is called Oaklash, a retro inspired bubbly font.

Taking after the name ‘Swarm’, a lot of the pages’s graphics are inspired by bees and other bee imagery such as honeycomb, flowers and hives. All graphics, including the logo are drawn by myself in a simple flat colour illustration style.
